Women Inspiration

While making this website, we were inspired by many women. Here are the few of the many.

Reshma Saujani

(shown on left), Founder of Girls Who Code. She has been such a great inspiration to us because she showed that being a female shouldnt stop you from what you want to accomplish.

Rose Broome

(shown of right), Creator of HandUp, an website that allows people to do direct donations to the homeless. Rose emphasized that not everyone lives in luxury and that some people have to deal with the lack of shelter and food.

Jess Ladd

Creator/Founder of Callisto, a project to help women who have been sexually assaulted. She has inspired us because women think less of themselves due to events that have experienced. They become traumatized from abuse and rape, which can leave scars that last for a lifetime that can affect their mental health. Her project detects where sexual predators are in the area.
